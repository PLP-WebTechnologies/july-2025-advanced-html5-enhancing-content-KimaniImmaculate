Why Learn Kenya Sign Language?

  1. Promotes inclusion and accessibility
  2. Improves communication with the Deaf community
  3. Enhances career opportunities in interpretation and education

Key Terms

KSL
Kenya Sign Language — the language used by the Kenyan Deaf community.
Interpreter
A professional who translates spoken language into KSL and vice versa.
Deaf Culture
The values, norms, and identity of the Deaf community.
